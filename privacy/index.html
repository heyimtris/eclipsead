our privacy policy

Welcome to Eclipse, a messaging platform developed and maintained by Tristan Wentworth, a solo developer ("we," "our," or "us").

Eclipse is a personal project created with a focus on privacy and user experience.

Your privacy is important to me, and this Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you use the platform located at eclipse.triswent.tech (the "Platform").

1. information we collect

When you use the Platform, we may collect the following information:

  • Your email address
  • Your username
  • Your password used to signup for the Platform
  • Your messages
  • Your profile information

1a, 1b, 1c, 1d and 1e are collected when you create an account and message people on the platform

If you want to learn more about the information we collect, please look at our Terms of Service

2. how we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide you with the Platform

    • We use your information to provide you with the Platform and its features.

  • Improve the Platform

    • We use your information to improve the Platform and develop new features.

  • Protect our users

    • We use your information to protect our users and prevent abuse of the Platform.

  • Comply with the law

    • We use your information to comply with legal obligations.

  • Enforce our Terms of Service

    • We use your information to enforce our Terms of Service.

  • Communicate with you

    • We use your information to communicate with you about the Platform.

3. how we share your information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent

    • If you explicitly agree to share your information.

  • With our service providers

    • We may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating the platform, such as hosting services or analytics providers.

  • With law enforcement

    • We may share information if required by law or to protect our users.

  • With our successors

    • If we are acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred to the new owners.

4. how we protect your information

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include secure servers and encrypting data before they're sent to the servers. Passwords are encrypted on our servers for added security. However, messages are not yet encrypted during the Alpha stage. Additionally, personal information may be accessed internally by us for operational purposes, but the public API will not have access to this data.

5. your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your information
  • Correct your information
  • Request that we delete your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Restrict the processing of your information
  • Object to the processing of your information
  • Request a copy of your information

If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at tris_went@proton.me

6. data retention

We will retain your information for as long as necessary to provide you with the Platform and comply with our legal obligations. If you would like us to delete your information, please contact us at tris_went@proton.me

The Platform may contain links to third party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites or services. We recommend that you review the privacy policies of these websites or services before providing them with your information.

8. children's privacy

The Platform is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with information, please contact us at tris_went@proton.me

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
